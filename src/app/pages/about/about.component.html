About

Alyssa James

I've been passionate about the entertainment industry since I was little. It started in dance, choir, and theatre, as I got older I found more interest in behind-the-scenes. In high school, I worked on the school news as a writer and editor. I also worked in the broadcasting booth on the news and at football games switching the live feed on our Jumbotron.
​
I graduated from Full Sail University in December 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Digital Cinematography. I've been extremely grateful to have worked on several different productions during my time at Full Sail, I've made a commercial, mini-doc, and several short films. 

Since graduating college I've been working on expanding my portfolio with personal projects as well as working with small creators trying to bring their videos to a larger audience with TikTok and Instagram reels. Currently, I am the Editor for the Do It For The Dogs Fellowship with WA2S.
​
I'm constantly perfecting the things I'm passionate about. I'm looking at everything with a different perspective, whether it's for photography, videoing, or a new way to edit something together.
